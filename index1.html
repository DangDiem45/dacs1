Black Panther: Wakanda Forever

Black Panther: Wakanda Forever is the final film in Phase 4 of the Marvel Cinematic Universe, following Thor: Love and Thunder - one of the worst Marvel movies of the decade. Therefore, it is highly anticipated, especially this is also a tribute to Chadwick Boseman, the actor who made the great success of Black Panther (2018), but unfortunately passed away from cancer. when season 2 was in the early stages of development.

Avatar: The Way of Water

The content of The way of water is simple, dividing the good and evil factions almost clearly. The topic is familiar, the contradiction is not new and there is no twist. However, the film is very attractive. The family theme - "old bottle, old wine" still makes viewers get caught up in every detail. The issue of environmental protection sounds like a big deal, but it is conveyed very gently and naturally. James Cameron loves nature and that love permeates every footage.

Action  Sci-fi  Fiction 2023 130 min

Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales

The film takes the story around a young girl reluctantly gets caught up in a summer vacation trip with her boyfriend. They decide to go on vacation to a small village in Sweden, but coincidentally this is also the time when the peaceful village is preparing to hold a special festival that happens once every 90 years. However, that event turned out to require a lot of blood and things no one could have expected. The film is filled with gruesome frames with organs, mysterious smiles, drawings of evil spirits and strange rituals that begin to appear, making the hairs on the back of viewers' hairs rise, standing still.

Action  Drama  Fiction 2022 128 min

Uncharted

This is a movie of the same name with a game title, revolving around the story of a teenager Nathan Drake about 20 years old who embarked on a treasure hunt adventure of Ferdinand Magellan. On his journey to find the treasure, Drake met Sully and Chloe all three started their life and death journey together. All three have to go through dangerous and thrilling pitfalls. Can Drake and his two new companions successfully find the treasure?

Action  Advanture  Secret 2022 116 min
